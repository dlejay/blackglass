Turn-key solar funding for commercial landlords

Less than 5% of commercial roofs in the UK are equipped with solar. This isn't an oversight; it's a massive, untapped economic opportunity. For decades, this potential has been locked behind significant financial complexity.

Property owners and landlords face prohibitive upfront capital costs, a confusing maze of subsidy applications, and rigid financing models that simply aren’t fit for purpose. The result is inertia—and a missed opportunity for both the property owner and the UK grid as a whole.

Blackglass Capital is built to solve this one problem. We are not a traditional lender; we are a dedicated finance partner. We have streamlined the entire process, from initial energy assessment to final installation, by removing these barriers. Our platform connects property owners with rapid, efficient capital, handling the complexities so you don't have to.

Our mission is to turn every commercial roof from an idle liability into a source of energy and stable, long-term revenue. We make solar funding so simple, there is no longer any reason to leave this potential untapped.
